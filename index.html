Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel poet

I have been accused of sedition. That is why I am now confined in the prison. On the one side is the crown, on the other the flames of the comet One is the king, sceptre in hand; the other Truth worth the mace of justice. To plead for me, the king of all kings, the judge of all judges, the eteralisation of a universal truth about mankind.
